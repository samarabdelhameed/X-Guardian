# X-Guardian: 4-Minute Professional Demo Script (English)

## Objective
Demonstrate the seamless integration of AI autonomy and X Layer blockchain execution by following a high-stakes "Emergency Protection" scenario.

---

### Phase 1: Authentication & On-boarding (0:00 - 0:45)
- **Step 1: Landing Page**: User opens the Cyber-Glow dashboard.
  - *Action*: Hover over the central AI Orb.
  - *Script*: "Welcome to X-Guardian. We are looking at a futuristic, obsidian-themed dashboard powered by X Layer. The central AI Orb is our heartbeat, representing the autonomous reasoning constant monitoring our funds."
- **Step 2: Connect Wallet**: Click the 'Connect' button in the top-right header.
  - *Action*: Trigger MetaMask, show the wallet address appearing.
  - *Script*: "First, I connect my Web3 wallet. X-Guardian identifies my address and immediately syncs my session with the X Layer Testnet. Notice the status bar—we are now live."

### Phase 2: Intelligence & Real-Time Monitoring (0:45 - 1:45)
- **Step 3: Navigating to Strategies**: Click 'Strategies' in the sidebar.
  - *Action*: Show the yield engine cards and the historical chart.
  - *Script*: "As a user, I want to see where my money is. X-Guardian aggregates real-time yields from DefiLlama. Here we see 'Morpho Guardian' yielding 23.6%. These aren't just numbers; they are live on-chain metrics."

  - *Action*: Show tooltips and toggle '1M' or 'ALL' ranges.
  - *Script*: "The UI is built for performance. I can toggle historical ranges instantly. X-Guardian analyzes this trend data to decide when a strategy is under-performing or at risk."

### Phase 3: The "Magic" Interaction (1:45 - 2:45)
- **Step 5: Command Center**: Go to Home. Click on the Magic Search Bar.
  - *Action*: Type 'status' and click the arrow.
  - *Script*: "I can interact with my portfolio using natural language. By typing 'status', I'm asking the AI Agent to perform a full audit. It checks the Block Number and Agent Wallet balance on X Layer in real-time. Look at the logs—everything is synchronized."

  - *Action*: Show the blue activity spinning.

### Phase 4: Emergency Mitigation (2:45 - 3:45)
- **Step 7: Identifying a Threat**: Type something into search (triggering radar) or click 'Execute Protection'.
  - *Action*: Click 'Execute Protection (Wallet)'.
  - *Script*: "Now, let's simulate a market emergency. I see a price fluctuation. I click 'Execute Protection'. This isn't a normal swap. X-Guardian is now preparing an atomic Multicall3 transaction."

  - *Script*: "Success! The transaction is mined. My portfolio is shielded. The AI reasoning engine logs the result, and my 'Missions' progress increases."

- **Step 10: Strategy Selection (Optional Preview)**: Click 'Invest' in the sidebar.
